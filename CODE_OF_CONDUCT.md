# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in AppShelfKit a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections.
- Keep feedback focused on the code, documentation, issue, or design.
- Be patient with contributors who are learning Swift, SwiftUI, or open-source workflows.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic comments after maintainers ask for focus.
- Abusive or spammy automation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable action to protect the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer. If a dedicated contact is added later, this file should be updated.
